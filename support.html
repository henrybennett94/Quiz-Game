Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I start the game?

A1: Click the "Start Game" button on the intro page to begin your adventure.

Q2: What happens if I answer a question incorrectly?

A2: You will be shown a "Wrong Answer" page where you can choose to restart the game.

Q3: Can I get hints?

A3: Currently, there are no hints available. Try your best to solve the puzzles on your own!
